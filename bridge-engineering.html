What I work on

Authorised bridge inspections

I provide authorised inspections of road-bridge objects, including visual examination, defect documentation, condition assessment, recommendations for maintenance or further diagnostics and formal reporting for owners and administrators.

Municipal bridge agenda

For public owners I can support the complete agenda of bridges, footbridges and culverts: evidence, bridge sheets, inspection intervals, maintenance manuals, budget planning and preparation of repair packages.

Drone reconnaissance

Where appropriate, I use a light drone for preliminary reconnaissance and safer visual overview of less accessible parts before detailed inspection.
