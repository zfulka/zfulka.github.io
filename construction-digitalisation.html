Construction digitalisation

Digitalisation in construction for me means practical workflows that reduce manual repetition and improve information quality: paperless delivery, 3D reinforcement modelling, script-based design in Python and parametric modelling.

What I work on

Paperless information workflows

I work with structured digital information instead of fragmented document sets. The objective is better traceability, fewer manual transfers and a stronger basis for coordination, quantities and downstream analysis.

3D reinforcement modelling

A useful digital model should not stop at generic geometry. Detailed 3D reinforcement modelling helps with understanding quantities, constructability and the preparation of more reliable asset data.

Script-based and parametric design

Python and parametric approaches can automate repetitive tasks, improve consistency and open the door to faster iterations, quantity extraction and better-controlled design logic.
