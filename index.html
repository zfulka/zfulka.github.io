BIM, sustainability and circular economy for transport infrastructure.

I help transport infrastructure organisations use BIM, structured data and lifecycle thinking to reduce material waste, improve information management and support more sustainable decision-making.

I am a civil engineer with long-term experience in bridge design, project management, BIM implementation and digital transformation in transport infrastructure. My work connects bridge engineering, information management, data standards, IFC-based workflows, carbon footprint assessment and circular economy principles.

My current professional and research interests include reducing construction and demolition waste, improving material efficiency, using BIM-based data for lifecycle assessment, and developing digital tools for sustainability assessment in transport infrastructure and research infrastructures.
