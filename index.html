About me

Professional profile

I help infrastructure and research organisations use BIM, structured information and digital workflows to improve delivery quality, reduce carbon and cost, and support more sustainable decision-making.

I am a project manager with over 20 years of experience in delivering complex technical and research projects, with strong focus on process optimisation and carbon / cost reducing workflows through standardisation and digitalisation. My work connects project management, BIM, graphical and data models, API / IFC / IDS integration, bridge engineering and sustainability assessment.

My current work spans transport infrastructure and research infrastructure: from digital standards and EN ISO 19650-aligned information management to circular economy, construction and demolition waste mitigation, and energy-chain optimisation for big-science facilities.
