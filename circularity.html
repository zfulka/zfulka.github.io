Circularity

I now combine Carbon & LCA with Circular economy under a broader Circularity theme: the emphasis is on material passports, future waste estimation, reuse and recycling pathways, and carbon-aware decisions across the asset life cycle.

What I work on

Material passports and WASTEie logic

The WASTEie-oriented approach links BIM objects with material identity, quantities, end-of-life scenarios and data suitable for future inventory and recovery planning.

Carbon and lifecycle perspective

Circularity is not only about waste. It also includes embodied carbon, whole life carbon and the comparison of decisions over the full life cycle of the asset.

Decision support

The aim is to help public owners and designers identify better material choices, lower-carbon pathways and clearer information for future demolition, reuse or recycling.
